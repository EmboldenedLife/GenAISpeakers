About Gen AI Speakers

Not just a club, but a movement combining communication with generative AI

Our Mission

Gen AI Speakers Toastmasters Club is positioned as 'not just a club, but a movement' combining communication with generative AI. Our mission is to explore AI tools (ChatGPT, Deepseek, Sora, image generators, voice assistants, AI agents) to amplify expression, engagement, and education.

We believe that the future of communication lies at the intersection of human creativity and artificial intelligence. Our club provides a safe space to experiment with these tools while developing traditional public speaking skills.

Our Vision

Innovation

Pioneer new ways of public speaking enhanced by AI technology

Community

Build a network of forward-thinking communicators

Education

Teach members to ethically and effectively use AI in communication

Meeting Structure

12:00 PM - Opening

Welcome and introductions with AI-assisted icebreakers

12:10 PM - Prepared Speeches

Members deliver speeches with AI-generated content or visuals

12:30 PM - Table Topics

Impromptu speaking with AI-generated prompts

12:45 PM - Evaluations

Feedback incorporating AI analysis tools

12:55 PM - AI Segment

AI News Anchor or Company Profiler presentation
